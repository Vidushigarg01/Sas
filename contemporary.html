Contemporary Classes


Contemporary dance is a genre of dance performance that developed during the mid-twentieth
century and has since grown to become one of the dominant genres for formally trained dancers throughout the world, with particularly strong popularity in the U.S. and Europe. Contemporary dance draws on both classical ballet and modern dance,
whereas postmodern dance was a direct and opposite response to modern dance.
